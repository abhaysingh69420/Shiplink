Our team didn't just design an app; we engineered a solution based on rigorous academic research methodologies.

### 1. The Root Cause (Ishikawa Diagram)
We used the **Ishikawa (Fishbone) Diagram** to identify why students struggle with international shipping. Key categories analyzed:
- **Environment:** Customs regulations and long waiting times.
- **Methods:** Chaotic Telegram/WhatsApp groups with zero accountability.
- **People:** Lack of trust when meeting strangers.
- **Costs:** High air cargo charges (â‚¬70â€“â‚¬90 for small items).

### 2. Strategic Assessment (SWOT)
- **Strengths:** 10x cheaper than DHL/FedEx; built-in HSRW student network.
- **Weaknesses:** Building trust for first-time users.
- **Opportunities:** Expansion to all major German "Uni-Cities."
- **Threats:** Complex customs laws (addressed in our Legal/Docs feature).
